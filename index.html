Price Is a Choice

How we choose prices affects how resources are allocated.

Price is a choice, nevertheless, the price system must always be considered holistically, and in the context of resource distribution.

So a system of prices always begins as a unitless system.

Interest: An Exchange Rate Across Time

The "real rate" is arbitrary.

What many people don't seem to realize, is that the choice of a "real interest rate", is arbitrary.

When we provide interest, we are exchanging money across time, or better stated, temporarily upgrading between money forms.
